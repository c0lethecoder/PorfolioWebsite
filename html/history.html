The History of Basketball

Basketball, one of the world's most popular sports, was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith, a Canadian physical education instructor. Originally played with a soccer ball and peach baskets, the sport has evolved into a global phenomenon with millions of players and fans worldwide. From the formation of the NBA in 1946 to the expansion of international basketball, the game has seen countless legendary moments, groundbreaking players, and dramatic transformations. On this page, we delve into the origins of basketball, its rapid rise in popularity, and how it continues to evolve, both in terms of the rules and the culture surrounding the game.
